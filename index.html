About

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow along with his brothers Cleon Barrow and Fenton Barrow.

The team is committed to promoting authentic music and Caribbean culture while giving a platform to emerging artists and creators.

They are actively involved in music production, recording, creative direction, and artist development, while engaging local communities and connecting Belizean talent to a global audience.
