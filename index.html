About

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow along with his brothers Cleon Barrow and Fenton Barrow. The team is committed to promoting authentic music and Caribbean culture while giving a platform to emerging artists and creators.

The Barrow brothers are actively involved in music production, recording, creative direction, and artist development. They work tirelessly to maintain originality, quality, and cultural relevance in every project.

Str8 Flight Entertainment also engages with local communities through events, workshops, and collaborations, aiming to inspire and nurture Belizean talent while connecting it to a global audience.
